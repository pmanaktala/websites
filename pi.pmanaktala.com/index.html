About Us!

A warehouse management system is a software application, that uses hardware designed to support and optimize warehouse or distribution center management. They facilitate management in their daily planning, organizing, and controlling the utilization of available resources, to move and store materials into, within, and out of a warehouse.
Planning - Finalizing the daily plan for receiving products, selecting the workload/orders to be processed in the day or shift.
Organizing - Keeping a track of the quantity of orders received and a count of the products that are coming in or going out.
Staffing - assigning staffs to different work each day.
Controlling - providing milestones for management to monitor progress through the day, providing the opportunity to respond to problems in a timely way and report data for performance analysis.
